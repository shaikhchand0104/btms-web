Bank Transaction Management System - Admin Panel

This is an admin-only banking management application. Please log in with your admin credentials to manage customer accounts and transactions.

Available Features:

  • Create new customer accounts
  • Deposit and withdraw funds
  • Transfer funds between accounts
  • Account transfers (Savings ↔ Current)
  • View mini statements
  • View all customer details and account information
  • Close customer accounts

Please login as admin to access the application.
